## Entry Points for Contributors

**Start anywhere. Fork freely. No permission needed.**

### Story Entry Points

- Write a scene where Lucius must use his Roman administrative skills to solve a local dispute, revealing the clash between imperial logic and tribal custom.
- Draft a letter Lucius writes but will never send, describing the taste of British bread to a friend in Rome.
- Create a monologue for Elen, explaining to her children who the quiet Roman is and why she brings him stew.
- Imagine a chapter from the perspective of Vellocatus the blacksmith, detailing his private thoughts on the 'senator's man' and the fragile economy of trust in Londinium.

### Technical Entry Points

- Build a 'Mud & Memory' text generator that blends descriptions of manual labor with flashback prompts, mimicking the novel's style.
- Create an interactive map of Lucius's Londinium, tagging locations with sensory details (smells, sounds, textures) from the text to explore the exile's phenomenological experience.
